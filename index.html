Shimla

Shimla and Manali are two popular hill stations located in the northern part of India. Here is a suggested itinerary for a trip to Shimla and Manali:

Day 1: Arrive in Shimla

Check into your hotel and rest for a while. In the evening, take a walk along the Mall Road and explore the local markets.

Day 2: Shimla Sightseeing

Visit the Jakhu Temple, situated on a hilltop, offering panoramic views of Shimla. Next, visit the Vice Regal Lodge, a colonial-era building with beautiful architecture and gardens. Take a stroll at the Glen Forests and soak in the natural beauty of the place. In the evening, you can watch a movie at the Gaiety Theatre or attend a cultural event at the Ridge.

Day 3: Shimla to Manali

Spiti

Spiti Valley is a high altitude cold desert mountain valley located in the northern part of India, in the state of Himachal Pradesh. Here is a suggested itinerary for a Spiti tour:

Day 1: Arrival in Shimla

Arrive in Shimla and check into your hotel. Explore the local markets and the Mall Road.

Day 2: Shimla to Sarahan

Check out of your hotel in Shimla and travel to Sarahan by road (around 5 hours). Visit the Bhimakali Temple, a famous temple dedicated to Goddess Bhimakali. Check into your hotel and rest for the day.

Day 3: Sarahan to Sangla

Check out of your hotel in Sarahan and travel to Sangla by road (around 4 hours). Visit the Kamru Fort and the Sangla Valley. Check into your hotel and rest for the day.

Day 4: Sangla to Nako

Leh

Day 1: Arrival in Leh

Arrive in Leh and check into your hotel. Take it easy to acclimatize to the high altitude.

Day 2: Sightseeing in Leh

Visit the Shanti Stupa, a Buddhist white-domed stupa located on a hilltop with panoramic views of Leh. Next, visit the Leh Palace, a former royal palace overlooking the town. In the evening, explore the local markets and try some traditional Ladakhi cuisine.

Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley

Check out of your hotel in Leh and travel to Nubra Valley by road (around 4 hours). Visit the Diskit Monastery, a 14th century monastery known for its statue of Maitreya Buddha. Take a camel ride in the sand dunes of Hunder. Check into your hotel or campsite in Nubra Valley and rest for the day.

Day 4: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake
