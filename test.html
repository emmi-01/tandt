Manali

Manali is a popular tourist destination located in the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Here is a sample itinerary for a 3 night 4 day trip to Manali: Day 1: Arrive in Manali and check into your hotel or resort. Day 2: Visit the Solang Valley, which is known for its adventure activities such as paragliding, zorbing, and skiing. Enjoy the picturesque views of snow-capped mountains and lush greenery. Shimla

Shimla and Manali are two popular hill stations located in the northern part of India. Here is a suggested itinerary for a trip to Shimla and Manali: Day 1: Arrive in Shimla Check into your hotel and rest for a while. In the evening, take a walk along the Mall Road and explore the local markets. Day 2: Shimla Sightseeing Visit the Jakhu Temple, situated on a hilltop, offering panoramic views of Shimla. Next, visit the Vice Regal Lodge, a colonial-era building with beautiful architecture and gardens. Take a stroll at the Glen Forests and soak in the natural beauty of the place. In the evening, you can watch a movie at the Gaiety Theatre or attend a cultural event at the Ridge. Day 3: Shimla to Manali Check out of your hotel in Shimla and travel to Manali
